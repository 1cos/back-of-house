Finishing a task / Terminar una tarea

Made it?
Tap Done.

When you finish a prep, tap Done. Brigade will ask how much you made — enter the quantity and confirm. That's it. Your production is logged.

Cuando terminas una prep, toca Done. Brigade te preguntará cuánto hiciste — ingresa la cantidad y confirma. Listo. Tu producción queda registrada.

Focus Mode

Shift starts.
Brigade locks in.

When you're scheduled to work, Brigade enters Focus Mode automatically. The app shows only your station — nothing else to click, nothing to get distracted by.

Cuando estás programado para trabajar, Brigade entra en Modo Focus automáticamente. La app muestra solo tu estación — nada más en qué hacer clic.
